Guess what? There is none! You never get it done (your passion – which can be your start-up) and you never get it wrong. And the reason you never get it wrong is because you never get it done – you can always improve and be better. Non-decisive state waters down the passion and you ask “Dude! I was so passionate about it; but not anymore.” Just make a decision – making one is important than making a right one or best one. You have the ability to make any decision the right one.
