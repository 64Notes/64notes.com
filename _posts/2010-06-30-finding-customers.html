Guess what? In this world where they cold call to make you buy your 10th credit card, I can’t have one. Reason? They are looking for customers in all the wrong places. Well someone could say that I have not tried enough and I might agree but that’s what an entrepreneur sees and fixes innit? Everyday, you lose me as your customer, and Shiraz – who is a close friend and Jane who is Shiraz’s girlfriend. You loose! Don’t go where the world goes to look for customer, go where the customer needs your service the most. I need you to be there where they just accept credit card (God knows why), where I just need you and no one else. I will say, “I love you!” It’s so much more effective than calling me when I am chatting with my girlfriend. I will say “F*ck Off!” You choose.
