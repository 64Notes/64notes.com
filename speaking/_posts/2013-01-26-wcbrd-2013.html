WordCapms are filled to the brim with WordPress users, developers, some aspiring developers and most of all - Bloggers! Hi fellas!. So imma take up the honorable task of helping fellow bloggers fix some basic UX problems.

My talk covered the following:

  1. Readability: Blogs are mostly written content, yet most of them (even the big guys like Wired Magazine) fail to present it properly. You can write the best shit in the world; but if people can't read it. It's a waste.
  2. Layouts: Thanks to themes, people present irrelevant unwanted information.
  3. Kill the Sidebar: Just focus on the content. Or at least use them wisely!
  4. Advertisements: Do not disturb! Place them where users click, change them randomly, mix them up. Just don't disturb the readers from main event - the content.
