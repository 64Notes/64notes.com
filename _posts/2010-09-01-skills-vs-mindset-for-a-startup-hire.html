--- layout: post title: Skills vs. Mindset - What's more important for a Startup Hire? status: publish type: post published: true --- When looking for hiring people for your startup, what is more important - skills or mindset? Skills can be taught but not mindset. In fact, they (co-workers) will learn most of the skills they need after they have joined. So a learning mindset is more important. They will need to cope up with ever-changing decisions and directions, so having patience and enthusiasm is necessary. Degrees, marks, resume cannot tell you about the mindset. Often those are written by someone charges few buck a page to type. They are found at unusual places, finding it hard to fit-in and unsatisfied with what is. They want more challenges, they are not hard to spot - just keep your eyes, ears and mind open.
