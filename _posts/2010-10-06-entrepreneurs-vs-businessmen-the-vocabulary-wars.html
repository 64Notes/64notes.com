'#Entrepreneurs v/s Businessmen: The Vocabulary Wars!' thesis_post_image: http://files.64notes.com//evil-businessman.jpg thesis_post_image_alt: Evil Business man pretending to be Entrepreneurs --- There are entrepreneurs, there are businessmen and then there are those who think themselves to be entrepreneurs while they are businessmen. Usually because they think it's cool. Or just want to teach entrepreneurs otherwise. It's easy to spot them both. Watch out for the vocabulary

Entrepreneurs use variations of:

  • Bootstrapping
  • Startup
  • Ideas
  • Discussions
  • Version 1
  • Shipping

Businessmen use variations of:

  • H.R.
  • Market
  • %
  • Margins
  • Meetings
  • Some big number shit that one gets or remembers except them
Just saying!
