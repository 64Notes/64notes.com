--- layout: post title: What is the Right Time to Do Big, New or Awesome Things tags: - Awesome - Time status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' thesis_post_image: http://files.64notes.com/now-is-the-right-time-snooze-clock.jpg thesis_post_image_alt: Now is the Right Time to do Good Things --- While expanding my personal notes to these blog posts, I often come across a note which is relatively a new learning. I often catch my self saying, “I can’t write about that. I need to practice it and learn more about it.” Or “It’s a new idea. I should wait.” Or even worse, “It’s such a good idea. I will blog about that later.” Many of us want to put awesome / new things off for later – for the right time. When are you gonna start-up? Later, when the time is right. When are you starting to blog? Not now, it’s going to be good – I am not feeling like it. … and on it goes. It’s fun to challenge that later and do it now! You will be amazed at how good you are. I believe there is no right or wrong decision; you have the ability to make any decisions the right one. Extension: There is no right or wrong day for anything; you have the ability to make any day the day. If you think it’s today – you are right. If you think it is tomorrow – you are right. Is it today? Bonus:
  • “If you think you can do a thing or think you can't do a thing, you're right." -Henry Ford
  • Why Now is the time to “Crush it!”
