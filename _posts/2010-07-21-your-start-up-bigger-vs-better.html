Innovate and Specialize your Business --- It’s crazy how every start-up wants to be bigger. Usually when I see them presenting their start-ups, there is a lot of talk about ‘scaling’ and how big the market is. It is one of the biggest turn-offs. Start-ups don’t grow up to be bigger ventures but to be better ventures. Don’t go big – specialize, innovate and rule the world. I’d rather have my web-designs start-up to grow into UI for shopping cart design agency than we-do-it-all design agency.
