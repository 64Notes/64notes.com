--- layout: post title: Who will Speak for You? tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' thesis_post_image: http://files.64notes.com//shut-up-or-speak-up.jpg thesis_post_image_alt: Speak up or Shut up _twitterrelated_short_url: http://bit.ly/avokKV _twitterrelated_short_urlHash: 06bfb37f1b37c5d1ea9132a35ad6c71f --- As a kid, I always thought that I will tell all those people who are wrong – when I grow up / when I have a voice. I will appreciate and award those whom I really love – when I grow-up / when I have a voice. I am not alone, a lot of us thought that as kids. Surprisingly, most of us are sill waiting to grow-up and for that voice. Let me tell you what, the voice is not anyone's to give you, the growth is not anyone's to assert on you. You get to choose. You get to choose when you speak-up, you get to choose when you are heard. You get to choose when you grow-up. You can choose. Let it be today – when you speak-up. All it takes is a tweet, a blog or just an email. It's today and now – or never. Speak-up what you believe in because no one else will.
