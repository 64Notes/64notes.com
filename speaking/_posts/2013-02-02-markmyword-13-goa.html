Mark My Word - India's first content & design conference. It was the best chance to introduce engineering students to the design world. Not just as a subset of Computer Science but as a way of thinking. My talk mostly covered:

  • Explanation of What design is in context with Art & Engineering.
  • What User eXperince Design is
  • Examples of Good & Bad Designs
  • Brief glimpse into two sub-sets of design: Information Architecture & Typography.
  • Brief about career as a designer.
  • Sheer Awesomeness
