64Notes began as an experimental online dump for some of my notes about entrepreneurship, startups, business, and (eventually) design. Just the act of rewriting the note sometimes lead to notes becoming essays.

Now 64Notes has become a timeline of my thoughts. Some of the old writings bring smile, laughter and, at times, even clarity. I intend to keep adding new ones once-in-a-while. Also, then there's a list of the talks I've given.

I solve dragon design problems at Instamojo. I've been working with/at startups since I was 16 something. You can find more about me here.

Since my first post in May, 2010, this little website has come a long way. It's often been on the front page of Hacker News and has been featured in mainstream & alternate media.
