Businessmen meet under the label of Entrepreneurs. But it's so easy to tell the difference. Ask them what their passion is and businessman tells you that it's money. Forget it dude. If you are after money, you are not entrepreneur. Entrepreneurs are after passion and money is not passion (maybe it is obsession). Passion is independent of outside factors (like money, fame and all) it's an inside thing. The day you discover it is the first day of you being an entrepreneur.
