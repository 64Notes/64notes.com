Porn is important. Damn important! Almost every teen's early life revolves around it. But online porn has much more to give than a momentary pleasure & relief. Our behaviour with porn sites, and their design for us can teach us a lot about user experience design.

Please note that this is a very basic idea and I decided to present it in a very early stage.
